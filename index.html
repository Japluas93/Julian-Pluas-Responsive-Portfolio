About Me

Hi there! My name is Julian, and yes! You are absolutely correct, I am a full-stack web developer. Actually no, I am not. However, I am planning to be someday and I am one step closer. I am currently a student at UCLA Extension's Coding Boot Camp. I just finished my third week where we covered some basic fundamentals of JavaScript. It doesn't look like a super complex language but I wouldn't go as far as saying that it is super easy to understand. It's definitely going to take some practice to get comfortable working with it.

My experience in this bootcamp has been mostly good so far. I have met some really cool people in my class. It seems like most of us are facing similar challenges. So it's definitely a common experience for students that enroll in this program. I think most of us are doing the same thing, just taking it one day at a time.
